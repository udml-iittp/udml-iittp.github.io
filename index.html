About Our Lab

Welcome to the Urban Dynamics and Mobility Lab, a forward-thinking research group within the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences at the Indian Institute of Technology Tirupati. Our work encompasses urban and transportation planning, bridging evidence-based policymaking, rigorous theoretical frameworks, and advanced data-driven techniques to foster sustainable growth. We study how policy interventions, infrastructure transformations, and technological innovations intersect with broader social, economic, and environmental factors to shape urban futures.

By exploring these integrated dynamics, our research seeks to enhance resilience, improve quality of life, and promote equitable access across diverse communities. Collaborating with government agencies, local stakeholders, and international partners, we strive to generate actionable insights that inform strategic planning and cultivate inclusive, future-ready cities. Ultimately, our goal is to support thriving urban ecosystems that adapt responsibly to evolving challenges and opportunities.
