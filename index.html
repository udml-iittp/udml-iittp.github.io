About Our Lab

Welcome to the Urban Dynamics and Mobility Lab at the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences, Indian Institute of Technology Tirupati. Our research focuses on understanding and improving pedestrian infrastructure, urban mobility, and climate-adaptive streetscapes using cutting-edge data analytics.

We blend spatial analysis, behavioral data collection, and advanced modeling to inform equitable and resilient urban environments.
